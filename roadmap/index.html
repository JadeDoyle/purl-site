Roadmap

What Purl does today, what is coming next, and the bigger ideas for later. This mirrors the Roadmap screen inside the app.

In the app

Everything Purl already does.

Patterns library
tag, categorise, search and sort your patterns
Pattern reader
follow along with row highlighting, ticking and saved progress
Pattern editor
write your own in sections, with abbreviations and a photo
Import shared patterns
open a .purlp file or a PDF with one button
Match yarn and patterns
find a pattern for a yarn you own, or stash yarn for a pattern, and pin a pattern to the exact yarn it calls for, by name
Projects
yarn, dye lots, sizes, dates and photos; the yarn you use comes off your stash
Pick up where you left off
the patterns you last knitted from sit at the top; opening a pattern starts a project for you
Filter projects
by status, craft, or the year you finished them
Link a pattern by URL
a project can point at a pattern that lives online, and one tap opens it
Project timeline
Project photo gallery
Record your actual gauge
Yarn weigh-in
weigh a ball to update how much you have left
Yarn stash
colour wheel, dye lots, gauge, totals, value in kroner, sorts and filters
Multi-colour yarns
self-striping, variegated and gradient yarns shown as stripes
Equipment stash
needles and hooks by size, material and tip shape
Shopping list
flag yarns to buy more of, share the list for a shop trip, and a heads-up when a colourway mixes dye lots
Nordic yarn catalogue
pick a brand and yarn to autofill weight, fibre, gauge and colour, with over a thousand shades from the main Nordic brands
Scan a yarn ball-band
scan the barcode to add a skein or prefill a yarn, and share your scans with other knitters
Colour picker
wheel or square, hex entry, and an eyedropper to lift a colour from a photo
PDF pattern viewer
open pattern PDFs (even very large ones) and pinch to zoom with room to write in the margins; works fully offline
Draw, highlight and erase
pens and a marker with presets, undo and redo
Neat shapes
hold to straighten a line or snap a clean circle or box
Lasso and move drawings
Sticky notes and on-page text
Bookmarks
Pinned snippet windows
keep a chart key or table floating while you read elsewhere
Find and navigate
page thumbnails, go-to-page, and full-text search of the pattern
Night reading
invert the page for evening knitting; your drawings keep their colours
Ruler
Row counters
name several, set a target, count up, down or by twos, with reminders at chosen rows
Chart stitch tracker
box a chart and step through it stitch by stitch: choose the start corner, alternate rows, keep several charts, jump to any spot
Calculator and glossary in the reader
reach both without leaving the pattern
Customisable tool button
drag the floating button anywhere and choose which tools it shows
Keep the screen awake while reading
Knitting calculator
gauge, resizing, even shaping, skeins needed, gauge from a swatch
Quick counters
stand-alone named counters: hold to count fast, set a target, drag to reorder
Terminology glossary
knit and crochet abbreviations in Norwegian and English, with US/UK notes
Tap a term to expand
plain-language explanations and examples for the trickier ones
Search everything
one box across projects, patterns, yarn, equipment, notes and the glossary, and accents don't matter
Backup and restore
everything to a single file, settings included
Recently deleted and history
a 30-day bin and dated snapshots to undo mistakes
Open Purl files from your file manager
Norwegian and English
switch language anytime
Dark mode
light, dark or follow the system
Made for iPad
a two-pane layout on big screens, with a text-size setting
Photo gallery
projects and your own written patterns hold a gallery of progress photos; browse them all in one place, pinch and swipe
Support Purl
Purl stays free. Leave an optional in-app tip if it has helped you, or share it with a friend
User guide
a full written guide, opened from More in your browser

Up next

The next things being worked on.

More yarn colours
Restore the Hillesvåg range and add Viking Garn; the remaining brands fill in from ball-band scans over time.
Chart tracker polish
Further refinements to the chart stitch tracker from real iPad and phone use.
Apple Pencil polish
Precision + palm-rejection check on a real iPad, and a stylus-only mode (finger pans, pencil draws).
Landscape drawing side-rail
A side rail for the drawing tools when the iPad is wide, folded into a floating-button rework.
