User guide

How to use Purl. Plain language, no code, no assumptions about programming or technical background.

This is the written companion to the in-app help (empty states, first-run tour, tooltips). Read here if you want to sit down and learn; everything inside the app gives you the same answers in the moment you need them.

Start here

  • Getting started: install, the four tabs at a glance, adding your first yarn and your first pattern and your first project.

The everyday loop

  • Yarn stash: adding yarn, colours, dye lots, multi-colour yarns, fibre composition, weighing yarn as you use it.
  • Patterns: importing a PDF pattern, writing your own, sharing patterns with friends.
  • Projects: starting a project from a pattern, picking yarn from your stash, the project gallery, marking finished.
  • PDF tools: row tracker, counters, the ruler, drawings, sticky notes + bookmarks, clearing annotations.

Powerful extras

  • Barcode templates: scanning ball-bands to fill in yarn details automatically, the catalogue, sharing your template library.
  • Backups + recovery: local snapshots, Recently Deleted, full backup files (.purl), how to move your data to a new device.
  • Terminology glossary: Norwegian and English knitting + crochet abbreviations, side by side or one at a time.
  • Calculator: gauge swatch, stitch resize, even shaping, skeins needed.

Reference

  • FAQ: known limitations, common questions, things that look like bugs but aren't.
