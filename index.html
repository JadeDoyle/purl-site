Offline and private

Patterns and PDFs

Follow along, row by row

  • Import pattern PDFs and open them offline, even the big ones
  • Draw, highlight and add sticky notes right on the page
  • Counters and a chart tracker follow you while you work

Projects

From cast-on to finished

  • A visual timeline of everything you are making
  • Progress photos and the gauge you actually got
  • Pick yarn for a project straight from your stash

Yarn stash

Every skein, accounted for

  • Colour, dye lot and fibre on every yarn
  • Scan the ball-band barcode and the details fill themselves in
  • Totals in grams and meters, at a glance
  • Often a paid extra in other apps. In Purl the stash is free, forever
