Press kit

Purl is a calm, private companion for knitters and crocheters. Projects, yarn stash, patterns and progress live in one place, free, offline, and stored on the user's own device, with no account and no ads. Purl speaks Norwegian and English, and is built to feel right on iPad as well as phones.

You are welcome to use the material below when writing about Purl. Please keep the logo's colours and proportions as they are.
